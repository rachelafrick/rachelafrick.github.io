When You're Stuck

Rachel Africk

November 5, 2020

12:00pm

We've all been there: You are trying to solve the latest crossword and you simply cannot think of anything else you know. One trick I have for these moments is trying to fill in letters, even if not words. For example, does the clue imply that the answer will be plural? Fill in an 's' at the end! Is the clue in the past tense? See if you can end your word with 'ed'! Even though it may seem small, these letters can be key in providing hints to other words.

Another big hint is language. If the clue is in spanish, it's likely time to bring out your own spanish skills. Finally, it's important to consider that you may not be pronouncing the word as intended. Try to put the emphasis on a different syllable, and see if a new meaning is revealed to you. Especially later in the week, the writers will design tricks like this to throw you off of your game!
